Concert commemorating mrs. Nikolina Angelova, founder and honorary conductor of the FELS Orchestra.

FELS Brass Orchestra, conductor - Svetlana Galianova
Freedom Youth Choir, conductor - Mihaela Koteva

National School of Music "Lyubomir Pipkov", Sofia, Bulgaria

04.12.2019

The Concert was a real herth-warmer, commemorating Nikolina Angelova, the founder and conductor of the Orchestra for the amazing 40 years.
In that saturday afternoon, the hall of the National School of Music appeared to be too small for all of the former players and their children who came to say one big Thank You.
Wonderful music,
endless ovations.
In the end, the Orchestra doubled in size in order to play a piece together with the former players.
We wish you all the best, Mrs. Angelova!
Deep admirations for your great career!

Christmas Charity Concert

Elin Pelin, Bulgaria

19.12.2019

For the ninth edition of the Christmas Charity Concert in Elin Pelin the "Yanko Yankov" hall was full and the audience saw many different performers, including our friends from the FELS Brass Orchestra, comming from Sofia, Bulgaria. The small market, full with DIY decorations and home made food as well as the tickets for the event raised 5000 Euros for charity. Part of the funds will be spent for improving the access to the school for the disabled. The rest is to be given to the disabled kids to fund their rehabilitation therapies. The organizers thank every one who attended this evend and helped this Chrismas miracle come to life.

National Brass Orchestras competition

Botevgrad, Bulgaria

28.06.2019-30.06.2019

The competition spans 3 days and it has the goal of celebrating the long bulgarian traditions in the brass music genre as well as to help the city grow as a popular cultural and tourist destination. The 36th edition of the competition is organized by the Botevgrad County. The orchestras come from all over the country - Vidin, Vratsa, Gabrovo, Vetovo, Sofia and our honorable guests this year are Orchestra form Romania.
