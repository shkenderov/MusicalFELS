OUR TEACHERS

Nikolina Angelova

Director and Conductor

Graduated from the National Music Academy in Sofia, Bulgaria. Co-founder of the orchestra, conductor and director since 1975. Leads the orchestra to many international awards in Germany, Poland, Norway and the UK.

Georgi Asenov

Percussion Instruments

Graduated from the National Music Academy in Sofia, Bulgaria.

Co-founder of a satire theatre. Member of the “OM” band for 20 years. Has successful tours in Scandinavia in collaboration with Ognyan Videnov .Recently published an album with the “Space Trio” band in Portugal.

Petar Rizov

Trumpets and Alto Horns

Graduated from the Musical School and the National Music Academy in Sofia with French Horn. Currently plays the French horn in the Sofia National Opera. Part of the FELS Teaching Staff since 1987.

Mihail Mihaylov

Clarinettes and Saxophones

Graduated from the National Music Academy in Sofia and plays for 2 years in the master class. Works in the “Brass Orchestra of Sofia”, later in the symphonic orchestra of the Bulgarian National Radio.

Marin Stankov

Trombones and Tubas

Graduated from the Musical School and the National Music Academy in Sofia. Since 2009 plays first Trombone in the Bulgarian National Guards Unit Brass Orchestra. For twenty years plays in the “Stefan Stefanov” brass orchestra.

Our Lead Players

Hristo Dodov

Tenor Horn

Hristo Dodov graduated First English Language School in 2018. Currently he studies Law in Sofia University St. Kliment Ohridski. Hristo started playing tenor horn in 2015 and since then he has participated in numerous performances of the orchestra in Bulgaria and abroad.

Lilya Uzunova

Drums, Trumpet

Apart from being able to develop my previous musical experience, in the orchestra and especially during our annual summer trips I’ve created some of the most exciting memories and lasting friendships – which, by the way, is a great opportunity to try out many different instruments!
