Our Lead Players

Nikolina Angelova

Founder and Honorary Conductor

Graduated from the National Music Academy in Sofia, Bulgaria. Co-founder of the orchestra, conductor and director since 1975. Leads the orchestra to many international awards in Germany, Poland, Norway and the UK.

OUR TEACHERS

Svetlana Galianova

Director and Conductor

Svetlana Galianova is born in 1994 in Dobrich, Bulgaria. She makes her debut as a conductor with the Academic Symphonic Orchestra in 2015. In 2018 she graduates with a degree in Opera-Symphonic conducting, performing Shostakovich' Symphony no.9. Conducting "Symphonetta Vidin", she performs at numerous venues, including in the "Magura" cave.

Petar Rizov

Trumpets and Alto Horns

Graduated from the Musical School and the National Music Academy in Sofia with French Horn. Currently plays the French horn in the Sofia National Opera. Part of the FELS Teaching Staff since 1987.

Mihail Mihaylov

Clarinettes and Saxophones

Graduated from the National Music Academy in Sofia and plays for 2 years in the master class. Works in the “Brass Orchestra of Sofia”, later in the symphonic orchestra of the Bulgarian National Radio.

Emil-George Atanassov

Trombones and Tubas

Emil-George has graduated from The Guildhall School of Music & Drama in London and since 2017 he is a member of the Sofia Opera and Ballet orchestra. He has been an active participant in many masterclasses here in Bulgaria and abroad. His interests are to expand the brass playing traditions in the country by encouraging and guiding the young players.

Our Lead Players

Hristo Dodov

Tenor Horn

Hristo Dodov graduated First English Language School in 2018. Currently he studies Law in Sofia University St. Kliment Ohridski. Hristo started playing tenor horn in 2015 and since then he has participated in numerous performances of the orchestra in Bulgaria and abroad.

Lilya Uzunova

Drums, Trumpet

My name is Lily, I have been playing the trumpet in the FELS Brass Band for a little over two years. Apart from being able to develop my previous musical experience, in the orchestra and especially during our annual summer trips I’ve created some of the most exciting memories and lasting friendships – which, by the way, is a great opportunity to try out many different instruments!
