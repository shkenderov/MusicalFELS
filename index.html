Our Team
Doing it for fun, not for grades.

In our long history since setablished in 1975, the orchestra has won multiple awards all across europe-Bulgaria, Norway, the UK and most recently - Germany and Poland, just to name a few, our teachers have inspired us and a number of players went on to pursue music related careers - be it in university or in a band.

The Fels brass band is an annual participant of events like the 24th of May national parade for The day of Slavic Writing and Culture on the on the streets of Sofia, the annual FELS Chrismas Charity Concert and the annual national school orchestras competition in Varshets, Bulgaria.
